================================================================================
                         COMMON QUESTIONS
================================================================================

Q: Do I need one person per photo?
A: No! Photos with multiple people are fine. Each face is detected and
   labeled separately.

Q: How do I tag faces with names?
A: Run the data_labeler tool. It shows each detected face and you type
   the person's name. Super easy!

Q: Will 75% accuracy work?
A: Yes, that's acceptable! But with good training data, you can easily
   hit 90%+ accuracy.

Q: Can I use both Ollama and OpenAI?
A: Yes! You can use either or both. Ollama is free and local. OpenAI
   requires an API key but provides highest quality.
